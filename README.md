## 🎯 Project Overview

**Problem:** Manual AR collections across 800+ invoices creates inconsistent follow-up cadences, delayed escalations, and limited real-time visibility into Past Due invoice status.

**Solution:** Rules-based automated dunning system integrated with SAP ERP — triggering communications at Day 15 / 30 / 45 overdue milestones with Power BI dashboard for finance leadership.

**Domain:** Order-to-Cash (O2C) · Accounts Receivable · Collections Management · Finance Transformation
